About Me

My name is Brian, and I am a certified nutrition and health coach with a deep passion for empowering individuals to transform their lives through nutrition and lifestyle changes.

My journey into health and wellness began with my own personal experience. I was diagnosed with ulcerative colitis, an autoimmune condition that presented me with significant health challenges. Navigating the ups and downs of this condition was not easy, but it was also an awakening moment and turning point for me in my life. It led me to discover the profound impact that nutrition and lifestyle can have on our overall well-being.

Through my research and personal experimentation, I realised that food is not just fuel, but a powerful tool for healing. By making conscious dietary choices and embracing a holistic approach to health, I was able to manage my condition and reclaim my vitality. This transformation inspired me to become a coach, so I could share my knowledge and help others overcome their own health challenges, just like I done.

I understand first-hand the frustration and uncertainty that can accompany a health diagnosis. I believe everyone is very individual and has their specific challenges they would like to overcome. That's why my approach is compassionate and personalised. Whether you're dealing with a chronic condition, seeking to improve your energy levels, or looking to adopt a healthier lifestyle, I am here to support you every step of the way and understand the difficulties of not feeling yourself.

My mission is to empower you to take charge of your health and live a life of energy, balance and fulfilment.

How can I help?

As a nutrition and health coach, my goal is to empower you to achieve optimal well-being, enhance your vitality, and find balance in this hectic world, by increasing your energy levels and reducing stress on the body.

There are many ways to achieve this and through my one-to-one coaching approach we can build a personalised plan and provide the tools and support you need to make the appropriate lifestyle changes for your individual needs.

How can this be achieved?

Nutrition

- Improve gastrointestinal health & digestion (Incl. appetite, heartburn, reflux, pain, bloating, wind, constipation/diarrhoea etc.)
- Include more whole foods in the diet.
- Improve eating habits.
- Address dehydration issues.
- Address specific dietary restrictions or preferences.
- Nutrition for sport and exercise.

Stress Management

- Manage and reduce stress levels.
- Improve sleep quality and quantity.
- Develop coping strategies for daily stressors.
- Enhance overall mental well-being.
- Incorporate meditation or mindfulness practice.

Weight Management

- Set realistic weight loss or gain targets.
- Focus on body composition rather than just weight.
- Create a healthy relationship with food.
- Implement sustainable lifestyle changes.
- Address emotional eating habits.

Movement and Exercise

- Establish a regular exercise routine.
- Increase physical activity throughout the day.
- Train for a specific event, fitness goal or sport.
- Find enjoyable and sustainable forms of exercise.

Lifestyle and Habits

- Establish a consistent sleep schedule.
- Cultivate better time management skills.
- Develop self-care routines.
- Foster positive social connections.
- Reduce or eliminate unhealthy habits (e.g., smoking, excessive caffeine).
